=== Brushing Teeth ===
 1. Turn on water and wet the brush
 2. Put a pea of toothpaste on the brush
 3. Brush outer surfaces in circular motions (30–45 sec)
 4. Brush inner surfaces (30–45 sec)
 5. Brush chewing surfaces (30 sec)
 6. Gently brush the tongue (10–15 sec)
 7. Spit and rinse the mouth
 8. Rinse the brush and let it dry

=== Doing Laundry ===
 1. Sort clothes by color and fabric
 2. Check pockets and zippers, turn delicates inside out
 3. Load the drum up to about 2/3 full
 4. Add detergent and softener if needed
 5. Choose program and temperature
 6. Press start and wait until finished
 7. Take out clothes and hang/lay to dry

=== Making Coffee ===
 1. Boil water and let it cool to ~92–96C
 2. Grind beans medium (or prepare ground coffee)
 3. Preheat equipment (dripper/press/cup) with hot water
 4. Bloom coffee with a little water (30–40 sec)
 5. Pour remaining water to target volume
 6. Wait for extraction and serve
 7. Optionally add milk/sugar
 8. Enjoy

=== Cooking Omelet ===
 1. Crack 2–3 eggs into a bowl
 2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper; optionally milk/cream
 3. Whisk until smooth
 4. Heat pan and melt some butter/oil
 5. Pour the mixture; cook on medium heat
 6. Lift edges with a spatula to distribute mixture
 7. Add filling (cheese/herbs/veggies) if desired
 8. Fold in half and serve
